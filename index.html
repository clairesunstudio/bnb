Bright & Beautiful

Dedicated to unlock the potential of
"left-behind" girls in rural China by
taking them on an artistic journey

260 million migrant workers in China today left behind over 66 million kids in the rural area, most of them do not get to see their parents annually.

1 in every 5 Chinese children is "left-behind", totalling over the population of the UK.

Due to lack of care, guidance and
other resoursces, these girls are
more prone to LONELINESS,
become SCHOOL DROPOUTS,
be subjected to PROSTITUTION,
be forced into CHILD LABOR,
and suffer more ACCIDENTS,
than their counterparts.

PULSERA BRACELETS

Collaborating with the Pulsera Project and combining the two good causes together, building a bridge of communication between the girls in the villages and the rest of the world.

STORY-T

Embroidery pieces made by the girls are sawn onto Tshirts. With a QR code on the back of each shirt linking to the story of the girl who designed the embroidery. We sold them on the website and the profits all went to helping each girl buying textbooks.

WRITE HER A POSTCARD

We invited attendees of the event in Boston to write down their encouragement and appreciation for the young female embroidery workers whom we met in rural Yunnan in summer 2013. These postcards aimed to help the girls realize their self-worth, improve self-esteem.

SUMMER ARTS CAMP

Each summer we are returning to Yunnan to host and Arts Camp and connect with the girls in the rural schools there. Support us on Indiegogo and KickStarter and follow us on social media.
